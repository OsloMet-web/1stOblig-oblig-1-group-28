Back in France, I study at an engineering school named INSA, in Toulouse. What I really love about my school is the fact that there are residences on campus. I just have to walk for 2 minutes to go to class. This is amazing because I don't have to wake up early and take public transportation. This is also very convenient to meet friends and party because we all live on campus.

magat2683

In France I'm an engineering student at ESIEE-Paris, and what I love most about my school is everything that happens outside of classes. Firstly, the camaraderie among students is amazing. We're like a big family, always ready to help and support each other. Secondly, there are so many clubs and groups to join. Whether you're into building robots or starting a business, you'll find a club that fits your interests. It's a great way to learn and make friends. Lastly, we know how to have fun. Students throw awesome parties and organize galas. It's a great break from studying and a chance to celebrate together.

In France, I study IT engineering at a school named INSA. What I like the most about my school is that we have to take a sport course each semester. We also have the choice of the sport we want to practice, among a long list of different ones. During the week, I always look forward this training class. In my opinion, it's important to be physicaly active. It helps relieving stress and tension. It permits feeling part of a group by making new friends. Finally, for me, it helps achieving goals and developping self-improvement.
